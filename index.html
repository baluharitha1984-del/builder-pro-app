About Telugu Language

Telugu (తెలుగు) is a Dravidian language native to India. It stands alongside Hindi and Bengali as one of the few languages with primary official language status in more than one Indian state (Andhra Pradesh and Telangana). Famous for its melodious, vowel-ending words, it is often called the "Italian of the East".

Learning Tips

Start with the Achulu (Vowels), which represent basic vowel sounds. Then move on to Hallulu (Consonants). Use our tracing pad to master the distinct rounded aesthetic of Telugu scripts. Once comfortable, jump into the Quiz Challenge to put your memory to the test!
