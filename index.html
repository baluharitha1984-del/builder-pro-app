💡 Why Step-by-Step matters

Every double-digit multiplication utilizes the distributive property:

A × (B + C) = (A × B) + (A × C)

By splitting numbers into units, tens, and hundreds, we resolve complex math into single-digit solutions that are trivial to sum up.
