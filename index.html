Securing 8/8 Marks in Class X Graph Question

Follow this standard marking scheme utilized in AP, Telangana & CBSE Board Examinations:

  • 1
    Table of Values (2 Marks):

    You must choose at least 5-7 values of x and compute the corresponding y coordinates accurately.

  • 2
    Scale & Axis representation (2 Marks):

    Specify the scale on the top-right corner. Draw both X-axis and Y-axis with proper positive/negative markings and origin (0,0).

  • 3
    Plotting points & Connecting them (2 Marks):

    Plot each ordered pair. Connect them with a smooth freehand curve for a parabola or a scale-ruled line for linear equations.

  • 4
    Zeroes / Solutions declaration (2 Marks):

    State clearly where the plotted graph cuts/crosses the X-axis. These x-coordinates are the zeroes of the polynomial!
